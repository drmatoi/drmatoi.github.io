Join our Team!

We are always looking for people who want to learn new things, improve things or share their own knowledge!

We need modders!

We are looking for people who can mod themselves or help with it, the more people send us mods, the more mods we can share and make available to you! Join at t.me/moddingtool!

Why should you join us?

No obligations

There is no minimum time or period in which you have to be active. If you feel like helping or developing something, you can do it and if you don't, you don't have to. You are completely free.

Game together

In addition to developing together, we often arrange to play video games. Of course, every member is welcome!

Problems?

Problems arise. However, our team works and helps everyone and every time. If you have any questions or get stuck, you can always ask!

What do I need to be able to do?

It would be best if you can show different basics for the things you want to do. Whether it's HTML, Phyton or Javascript, everything is useful in our team. If you don't have any skills in this area yet, you can learn it for free with us!
